DataFest 2025: Real Estate Office Rental Strategy

Office rental strategy using large-scale U.S. Census data (500M+ records) with a Power BI dashboard to identify high-opportunity zones using geospatial and demographic signals.

What I did

  • Designed a dashboard experience optimized for decision-making (filters, drill-downs, KPI views)

Results

  • Delivered stakeholder-ready visuals and narrative insights
